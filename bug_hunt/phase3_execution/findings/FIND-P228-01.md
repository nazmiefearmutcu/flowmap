### Repro
1. Static: paint builds `QImage(buf.data, bw, bh, bw*4, Format_RGBA8888)` — Qt does not own pixels.
2. Writers rebind `_buffer`: `engine.resize` (`_buffer = new_buf`), `np.roll(...)` assignment on widget scroll/drag.
3. Risk path: nested event processing during paint (dialogs, `processEvents`) or retained QImage after rebind → UAF / garbled frame.

### Expected
Buffer lifetime owned for full QImage use: `.copy()` before draw, or pin buffer until `drawImage` returns and forbid rebind re-entrancy; assert C-contiguous RGBA.

### Actual
Zero-copy wrap is intentional for speed; rebind sites are same-thread but not guarded against re-entrancy. OpenGL backend may prolong texture upload risk (P2-25).

```1166:1172:flowmap/ui/heatmap_widget.py
                        qimg = QImage(
                            buf.data,
                            bw,
                            bh,
                            bw * 4,
                            QImage.Format.Format_RGBA8888,
                        )
```

### Fix hint
Use `QImage(...).copy()` for safety, or keep a generation counter and rebind-safe paint; assert `buf.flags['C_CONTIGUOUS']` and `dtype==uint8`.
