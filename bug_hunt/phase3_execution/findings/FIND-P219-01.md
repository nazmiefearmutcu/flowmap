### Repro
1. Start replay on large range; worker does `records = list(book_iter)` and `raw_trades = list(trade_iter)` with **no** `_running` check mid-materialize.
2. User hits Stop immediately → `stop()` sets `_running=False` but load continues until lists complete (OOM/long I/O possible).
3. `stop_replay`: `quit()` + `wait(5000)` then nulls `_worker`/`_thread` regardless of `isRunning()`.
4. After timeout: orphan thread may still put to shared queue (P2-13/22).
5. Blocking slot `run_replay` → `start_replay` holds thread; `quit()` ineffective until materialize+emit return (same class as live asyncio).

### Expected
Cancellable load (chunked iter + flag); on wait timeout retain ownership and refuse new start; no late puts after stop.

### Actual
Stop during load often waits full 5s then orphans; full materialize is architectural mem risk (R05 H6).

### Fix hint
Stream emit without full `list()`; check `_running` every N records; if wait times out do not null refs; block dual start.
