### Repro
1. Accumulate long `_history` (e.g. 2k–10k frames) with deep books.
2. Trigger full rebuild: Go Live (L), zoom row height, drag-release, ticks_per_row change.
3. Measure wall time around `rebuild_heatmap` (or observe UI freeze).

### Expected
Interactive rebuild budget roughly ≤50–100 ms p95, or chunked/off-main-thread with progressive paint.

### Actual
Main-thread path: history list copy → centering pre-pass over all slice entries → dense `bid_grid`/`ask_grid` fill → optional SciPy `gaussian_filter1d` (import inside method) → normalize + LUT write. Throttle (`request_rebuild_throttled`, 50 ms) limits **start rate only**, not duration. Callers of direct `rebuild_heatmap` (L/Esc, mouseRelease, price_zoom, resize via size-mismatch push) are uncapped.

### Fix hint
Instrument p50/p95; cap work (downsample history, drop smooth during interactive drag); optional worker + swap buffer; never stack multiple full rebuilds without cancel/coalesce by final view state.
