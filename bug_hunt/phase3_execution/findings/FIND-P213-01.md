### Repro
1. Open `flowmap/ui/source_manager.py` — `self._queue = queue.Queue()` with no `maxsize`.
2. Open producers: `crypcodile_live.py:179-187`, `crypcodile_replay.py:513-521`, `crypto.py:218-251` — all use blocking `put` with no drop/timeout.
3. Start replay at `speed=0` (max) on a dense `book_delta` lake, or stall GUI (long rebuild).
4. Observe `SourceManager.queue.qsize()` climb without bound while producer continues.

### Expected
Bounded queue and/or producer backpressure/conflation so RSS and lag stay within SLA under burst/max-speed replay.

### Actual
Process-lifetime unbounded FIFO. Producer rate is not coupled to consumer (`_gui_tick` ≤1000 msgs/tick). GUI stall or `running=False` without producer join → linear growth until OOM/jetsam.

### Fix hint
`Queue(maxsize=N)` with drop-oldest non-snapshot / conflate updates; or pause replay when `qsize>N`; never drop last snapshot. Pair with P2-14 drain policy.

### Evidence
- Static: `source_manager.py:82` `queue.Queue()` no maxsize
- Static: puts always succeed (`crypcodile_live.py:181`, `crypcodile_replay.py:515`)
- Static: consumer gated + capped (`main_window.py:896-910`)

### Fix (2026-07-13)
Phase-4: `DropOldestQueue(maxsize=50000)` in `source_manager.py`; worker `put()` non-blocking + drop-oldest. See `bug_hunt/phase4_fixes/FIX_TICK_QUEUE.md`.
