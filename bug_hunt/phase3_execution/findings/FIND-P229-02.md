### Repro
1. Grep `_needs_rebuild` under `flowmap/` (production path).
2. Confirm writes only in `density_engine.py` (init/reset/resize).
3. Confirm HeatmapWidget `push_snapshot` / paint never reads the flag.

### Expected
Flag either drives a full rebuild after partial resize or is removed.

### Actual
`resize` sets `self._needs_rebuild = True` but live widget ignores it; dead signal contributes to blank-history class (FIND-P229-01).

### Fix hint
In `HeatmapWidget.push_snapshot` (or after resize): if `engine._needs_rebuild`, call `rebuild_heatmap()` and clear flag. Or delete flag and always rebuild on resize.
